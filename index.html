Privacy Policy & Terms

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Data Transparency

NetVista Pro captures real-time signal metrics including RSRP (Reference Signal Received Power), RSRQ, SINR, and Cell ID. This data is processed locally on your device to stabilize connection parameters.

2. Location Usage

Access to precise location (GPS) and background location is essential to map the nearest cellular towers. This data is never shared with third parties for marketing.

3. Real-time Optimization

The "Speed Booster" feature works by identifying network congestion and refreshing the radio frequency (RF) link. No external servers are used to tunnel your traffic; your privacy remains intact.

4. Pro Membership & Refund

The Pro Activation Key provides lifetime access. Due to the digital nature of the product, payments are non-refundable once the key is issued. However, 24/7 support is guaranteed for technical issues.

5. Third-party SDKs

We use Google Firebase for crash analytics and Mapbox/OSM for tower mapping. These services handle data according to their respective privacy policies.

6. Security

Your Device ID is used only for license verification. We employ industry-standard encryption to protect any data sent during support sessions.
